# Voice

Get offline notifications by a phone call. This is a great option for people who are unable to receive SMS messages.

<Callout type="info">
Call requires a paid subscription and sufficient credits. 1 call notification costs 2 credits.
</Callout>

## Setup

1. Go to the [Spectate Incident Channels](https://app.spectate.net/incidents/channels) and select "Voice".
2. Enter your phone number in internationalised format (e.g. +31101234567) and click "Add Channel".
3. You can now send a test notification to make sure everything is working.
