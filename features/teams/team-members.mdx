To work together in Spectate, you can invite colleagues to your team.

<Info>
Managing team members is only available to the roles **Owner** and **Manager**.
</Info>

## Invite team members

To invite team members, go to **Team settings** and go to the **Members** tab. Click the **Add person** button on the top right and enter the email address of the person you want to invite.

<Note>
On the **Solo** plan, you can not add any team members besides yourself.
</Note>

## Remove team members

To remove team members, go to **Team settings** and go to the **Members** tab. Click 3-dots and then click the **Remove** button next to the person you want to remove.

## Change team member roles

To change team member roles, go to **Team settings** and go to the **Members** tab. Click 3-dots and then click the **Change role** button next to the person you want to change the role of.
