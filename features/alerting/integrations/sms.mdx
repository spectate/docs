---
title: 'SMS'
description: 'Get offline notifications by using SMS. This is a great option for people who don''t have a smartphone or who want to use SMS as a backup.'
---

<Note>
SMS requires a paid subscription and sufficient credits. 1 SMS notification costs 1 credit.
</Note>

## Setup

1. Go to [Integrations](https://app.spectate.net/integrations) and click on `Configure` in the SMS card.
2. Enter your phone number in internationalised format (e.g. +31101234567) and click `Save`.
3. You can now send a test notification to make sure everything is working.
