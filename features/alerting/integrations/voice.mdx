---
title: Voice
description: 'Get offline notifications by a phone call.'
---

<Note>
Call requires a paid subscription and sufficient credits. 1 call notification costs 2 credits.
</Note>

## Setup

1. Go to [Integrations](https://app.spectate.net/integrations) and click on `Configure` in the Voice card.
2. Enter your phone number in internationalised format (e.g. +31101234567) and click `Save`.
3. You can now send a test notification to make sure everything is working.
