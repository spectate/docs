title: Incident AI
description: 'AI-powered incident response with root cause analysis.'
---

Incident AI is a feature powered by AI to help you diagnose and resolve incidents faster. It uses machine learning to analyze your incident data and provide you with actionable insights, such as potential causes and their solutions.

<Info>
    Incident AI is only available for the **Pro** and higher plans.
</Info>

![Incident AI](/images/incident-ai-example.png)

## Feedback

Mark any of the suggestions as **Helpful** or **Not Helpful** to help Incident AI learn from your feedback. You can also add your own feedback to help Incident AI learn from your experience.

<Note>
Marking suggestions as helpful is currently in development.
</Note>
