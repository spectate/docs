---
title: 'Create a team'
description: 'Manage your monitors and servers together in a team.'
---

Once logged in, you will be prompted to create your first team.

Creating a team is as easy as giving it a name and optionally adjusting the team's timezone.

![Create a team screen](/images/team-onboarding.png)

## Received an invite instead?

If you received an invite, you don't have to create a team. Instead, you can accept the invite and join the team.
Do this by clicking on the link in the email and following the instructions.
